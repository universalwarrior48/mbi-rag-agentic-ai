### **Foundations of Generative AI**

- Explores the rise of generative AI and its impact on industries, highlighting the demand for skilled developers.
- Introduces essential concepts like prompt engineering and the LangChain framework for building AI applications.

- Delves into LangChain's core concepts, including chains, tools, and agents for creating responsive applications.

- Covers evaluating different language models to select the best fit for specific applications.
